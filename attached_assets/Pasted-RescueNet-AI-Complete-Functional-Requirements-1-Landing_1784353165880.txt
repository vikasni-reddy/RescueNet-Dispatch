30. End-to-End Workflow

The complete application workflow should be:

User submits an emergency report.
AI detects the language and translates it if necessary.
AI extracts structured incident information.
AI generates a concise incident summary.
AI calculates the priority score and confidence level.
The incident is displayed on the live map with a color-coded priority marker.
The system identifies and recommends the nearest suitable emergency resources.
The operator reviews the AI recommendations and dispatches a resource with one click.
The assigned resource and its route to the incident are displayed on the map.
The incident progresses through its lifecycle (Pending → Assigned → Dispatched → En Route → In Progress → Resolved → Closed).
Every action is recorded in the dispatch timeline and activity log.
The dashboard updates in real time with analytics, notifications, resource utilization, and incident status
